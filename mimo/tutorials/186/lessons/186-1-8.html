The Rail Fence cipher writes plain text downwards and diagonally on rails of an imaginary fence.

Let's try a three-row fence with "LET US HACK", shall we?

L . . . S . . . K. E . U . H . C .. . T . . . A . .

Good job! Now we can read the rows and get "LSKEUHCTA" as cipher text.
