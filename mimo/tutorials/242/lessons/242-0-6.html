There's more to that, though: between HERE IS MY INVITATION and ENOUGH TALK, we can perform as many arithmetic operations as we want.

IT'S SHOWTIME
HEY CHRISTMAS TREE myNumber
YOU SET US UP 10
GET TO THE CHOPPER myNumber
HERE IS MY INVITATION 3
GET UP 2
YOU'RE FIRED 1 2 3
ENOUGH TALK
TALK TO THE HAND myNumber
YOU HAVE BEEN TERMINATED 5 10 15

See that? The operations are processed in the order we write them. In this example, we're calculating 3 + 2 and multiplying the result.
