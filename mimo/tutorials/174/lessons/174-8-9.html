"Agent Smith" has a list of missions he needs to take care of after taking a well-deserved vacation. Let's make a loop to follow him as he completes them.

Create a list of missions. Go through the list and tick off missions by displaying them and removing them from the list.

var m = new List<string>();
m.Add("defeat weapons dealer");
m.Add("investigate hijacking");
m.Add("uncover jewel smuggler");
foreach (string mission in m)
{
 Console.WriteLine("Completed: "
  + mission);
 m.Remove(mission);
}

Welcome back, "Agent Smith"!
