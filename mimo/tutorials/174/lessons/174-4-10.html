Arrays are collections of values or variables of a single type.

int[] distances = { 100, 27, 35, 65, 90 };
Array.Sort(distances);
distances[0] = 15;
string s = String.Join(", ", distances);
Console.WriteLine(s);
15, 35, 65, 90, 100

We've created and populated and sorted them, checked their length and accessed and changed elements. Gosh, we're on fire!
