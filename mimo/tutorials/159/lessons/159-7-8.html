Replacing or updating a value is just as easy as adding something.

HashMap<Integer, String> map;
map = new HashMap<>();
map.put(23, "Michael");
map.put(91, "Dennis");

map.put( 23 91 , "Scottie");
System.out.print(map); {23=Scottie, 91=Dennis} {23=Michael, 91=Scottie}

In fact, it's the same thing.
