Did you know Ruby can handle multiple methods in a single statement? I feel like there's a grammatical error with my reversed name. Let's fix that!

name = "Truthy"
print name.reverse. capitalize upcase downcase 
"Yhturt" "YHTURT" "yhturt"

Much better! Since we chained the methods, Ruby reverses what's in name and then capitalizes it, in that order.

Psst: Ruby can also convert strings into uppercase or lowercase letters.
