The modules we're including in classes are also known as mixins. Let's include multiple mixins in this Sheep class!

def Sheep
 include Noise
 include Quadruped
 def number_of_legs
  Quadruped::LEGS
 end
end

sheep = Sheep.new
sheep. make_noise('Baaah') number_of_legs
