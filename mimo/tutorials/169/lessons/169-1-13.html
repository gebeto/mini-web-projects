How can we get task into this string?

task = "sleep"
like = "I like to  #{task} #task {task} in the dark"
print like
I like to sleep in the dark I like to #task in the dark I like to {task} in the dark

That's great, I think most people do! Remember that interpolation only works with double quotes and needs to be in between #{}.
