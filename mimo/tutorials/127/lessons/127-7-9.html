This time, "Sir Lancelot" gets into a fight with a villain. The hero's attacks deal 2 damage points and the villain has 8 health points.

Create a loop and have "Lancelot" attack for as often as it takes to defeat the foe.

let lancelotDP = 2
var foeHP = 8
while foeHP > 0 {
  foeHP -= lancelotDP
  print(foeHP, terminator:" ")
}
print("Victory!")
