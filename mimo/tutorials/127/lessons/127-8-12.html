Since his fight, "Sir Lancelot" hasn't gotten a lot of rest. In fact, the hero's health points are down at 2. Luckily, magic potions restore 2 health points.

Create a function to drink a potion and a loop that calls the function until "Lancelot"'s health points reach the safe zone of at least 10.

var lancelotHP = 2
func drinkPotion() {
  lancelotHP += 2
}
while lancelotHP < 10 {
  drinkPotion()
}
print(lancelotHP)
10
