Okay, let's talk about the comment tag, an odd-looking tag that helps us explain and document things.

<!doctype html>
<html>
 <body>
  <h1>My favorite things</h1>
  <p>
   Raindrops on <em>roses</em><br>
   Whiskers on <strong>kittens<strong>
  </p>
  <!--  -->
  <hr> 
  <p>These are a few of my favorite things</p>
 </body>
</html>
<html>
<body><h1>My favorite things</h1><p>Raindrops on <em>roses</em><br>Whiskers on <strong>kittens<strong></p><hr><p>These are a few of my favorite things</p></body>
</html>
]]>

See that? By writing something between <!-- and -->, we can add hints and reminders to the code that are ignored by the browser.
