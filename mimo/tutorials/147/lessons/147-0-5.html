Let's take a look at the <html> tags, which mark the beginning and the end of a webpage!

Can can arrange the tags so that they *surround* the tags?

<html> <body>
</body></html>

Sweet! The <html> tag, which starts with <html> and ends with </html>, is the container for everything that belongs to a webpage.

Think of a sandwich: everything that belongs to a web page needs to go between <html> and </html>.
