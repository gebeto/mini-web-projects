The <span> tag, on the other hand, helps us group inline HTML elements.

<!doctype html>
<html>
 <body>
  <div>
   <h1>A familiar story</h1>
   <p>A <span style=" color :red; text-align :center;">long time ago</span> in a galaxy far, far away ...</p>
  </div>
 </body>
</html>
<html><body><div><h1>A familiar story</h1><p>A <span style="color:red;">long time ago</span> in a galaxy far, far away ...</p></div></body></html>
]]>

Good stuff! <span> elements are a great way to add hooks to small parts of a text or web page that we want to format.
