Now that we know about the head section and what it's used for, let's use the <title> tag to give the webpage a title!

<html>
<head>
  <title> My webpage </title> </title>
</head>
</html>

Sweet! Now, when we open the webpage in the browser, it'll say "My webpage" in the title bar of the browser window.
