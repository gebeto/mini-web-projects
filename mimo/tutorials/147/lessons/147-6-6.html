Sometimes we need headings or paragraphs to be centered. The good news is that there's a property for just that: it's called text-align.

<!doctype html>
<html>
 <body>
  <h1 style=" text-align :center; margin :center;">I'm at the center of attention</h1>
 </body>
</html>
<html><body><h1 style="text-align:center;">I'm at the center of attention</h1></body></html>
]]>

Sweet! With text-align, we can specify the horizontal alignment of text in an HTML element.

Psst: text-align doesn't work so well for other HTML elements. No worries, though; we'll talk some more about alignment in the CSS micro-course.
