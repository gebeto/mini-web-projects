Have you noticed that <br> didn't have a closing tag? That's okay because it's an empty tag.

While tags like <p> can and should have content, empty tags can't and shouldn't.

<!doctype html>
<html>
 <body>
  <h1>My favorite things</h1>
  <p>
   Raindrops on roses <br> <br></br>
   Whiskers on kittens
  </p>
 </body>
</html>
<html>
<body><h1>My favorite things</h1><p>Raindrops on roses<br>Whiskers on kittens</p></body>
</html>
]]>

Nice! It's really important to remember that we use the <br> tag to add line breaks, not to separate paragraphs.
