What if we want to emphasize things in a web page? Well, there's a tag for that.

<!doctype html>
<html>
 <body>
  <h1>My favorite things</h1>
  <p>
   Raindrops on  <em>roses</em> <em>roses roses</em><br>
   Whiskers on kittens
  </p>
 </body>
</html>
<html>
<body><h1>My favorite things</h1><p>Raindrops on <em>roses</em><br>Whiskers on kittens</p></body>
</html>
]]>

Impressive! The <em> tag is a phrase tag for emphasized content. Phrase tags tell the browser that some content has a special meaning.

Remember rule #2: close every tag you open. That, by the way, doesn't include empty tags.
