There's also a phrase tag for important content: the <strong> tag.

<!doctype html>
<html>
 <body>
  <h1>My favorite things</h1>
  <p>
   Raindrops on <em>roses</em><br>
   Whiskers on <strong>kittens </strong> </em>  
  </p>
 </body>
</html>
<html>
<body><h1>My favorite things</h1><p>Raindrops on <em>roses</em><br>Whiskers on <strong>kittens</strong></p></body>
