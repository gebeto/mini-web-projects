Let's have a look at another type of loop! So-called while loops execute a block as long as a certain condition is met.

temperature = 90
summer = True
while for  temperature >= 60:
  temperature -= 1
summer = False
print(temperature)
59

Nice! While loops use the while keyword and a condition to determine the number of cycles, or iterations.
