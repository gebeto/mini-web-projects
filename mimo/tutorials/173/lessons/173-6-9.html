Loops execute blocks over and over again for as long as a certain condition is met.

for i in range(3):
  print("for loop #" + str(i))

bee = "bee"
while bee != "beeeee":
  bee += "e"
  print(bee)
for loop #0
for loop #1
for loop #2
beee
beeee
beeeee

A for loop iterates based on elements or a range. A while loop uses other conditions to define its duration.
