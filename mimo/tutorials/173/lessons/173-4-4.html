We can take this a step further and include if statements within other if statements. Make sure to keep track of the indentation!

testvar = 2
if testvar > 0:
  print("test more than 0")
  if testvar == 1:
    print("test just 1")
  else:
    print("test more than 1")
test more than 0
test more than 1

The second and third print() are indented even more than the first print(). Each conditional statement adds a level of indentation.
