If we want to exclude any records that have fields between certain values, we use the art of negation.

SELECT *
FROM people
WHERE age NOT BETWEEN 10 AND 50;
 id |   name   | age
----+----------+----- 
  3 | Lancelot | 800
  4 | Tom      | 75
  5 | Jerry    | 75
