How can we use patterns to find multiple fields in our table?

SELECT first_name, city FROM people
WHERE first_name LIKE '%ob' '_ob' ;
 first_name | city
------------+---------------
 Bob        | Atlantis
 SpongeBob  | Bikini Bottom  first_name | city
------------+---------------
 Bob        | Atlantis

That's it! % matches any characters that come before ob in this case.

That's not wrong, but we want multiple fields, and _ only matches a single character.
