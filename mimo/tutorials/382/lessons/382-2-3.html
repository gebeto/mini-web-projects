Find or make an square image of yourself and move it to your blog folder. Then add an &lt;img&gt; element within the author details section and add a src attribute that points to your image.

...
&lt;div class="post-author"&gt;
&lt;img src="me.png"&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
...

Psst: &lt;img&gt; elements don't have a closing tag.
